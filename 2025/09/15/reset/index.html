reset

Published on: 2025-09-15 06:43:54 by nylan

1 min read

i reset it all, started from scratch, made a new me.
did you ever play video games like, final fantasy, or azure dreams, or something with a path that could be optimized?
i had a hard time with that. i wanted to be perfect, to follow the exact path that got me the exact things.
and that meant i never ever got anywhere in a game.
and i suspect the same can be said of life.
